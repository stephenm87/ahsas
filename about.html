About AHSAS

Privacy, licensing, and project information

🔒 Privacy Notice

AHSAS is designed with student privacy as a top priority.

  • Minimal data collection: We only collect first names or initials and discussion ideas. No email addresses, no accounts, no login required.
  • Ephemeral data: All student submissions are temporary. Data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of the activity session.
  • No tracking: We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technology.
  • No data sharing: Student ideas are never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party beyond the AI synthesis service (Google Gemini), which receives only the anonymous text content — no names, no identifiers.
  • Local-first: Student form drafts are saved locally in the browser only and are cleared after submission.
  • No persistent storage: After the teacher synthesizes the guidelines, only the final output (which contains no individual student data) can be saved. All raw submissions are ephemeral.

📋 Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. All contributors and users are expected to:

  • Use welcoming and inclusive language
  • Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
  • Accept constructive criticism gracefully
  • Focus on what is best for the classroom community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, publishing others' private information, or any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in an educational setting.
